UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS FOR GITHUB PAGES

1. Upload the CONTENTS of this folder to the ROOT of your GitHub repository.
2. Your repository root should contain index.html, css/, js/, images/, about/, packages/, booking/, contact/, faq/, service-areas/, favicon files, robots.txt, sitemap.xml, and site.webmanifest.
3. To remove .html from navigation, delete old root files such as about.html, packages.html, booking.html, contact.html, faq.html, and service-areas.html from your repository after uploading these folders.
4. Keep your own real photos inside the images/ folder. Do not delete your images folder.
5. After upload, open your live website and press Ctrl + F5.
6. Submit https://sunshinedrivingcentre.com.au/sitemap.xml in Google Search Console after your domain is live.
